02 — The Cluster Guide

Each region earned its reputation.

Years of placements taught us which capabilities concentrate where — so a search starts in the right city, not with a blank map.

Primary

Mexico — the anchor

The region's largest pool of accountable, multi-hat engineers. Monterrey's business culture points north to the US; Mexico City is LatAm's startup flagship; Guadalajara runs 100-person-scale dev operations; Colima is a micro-hub of ~20 software firms serving US clients. Ruby and full-stack strongholds trace back to the companies that grew there.

Core

Colombia

English proficiency on par with Mexico and a deep, competitive bench — our first stop when a search needs breadth beyond Mexico at the same quality bar.

Core

Argentina

Excellent English and a strong mix of engineers across disciplines — a reliable source of senior, client-facing talent.

Core

Costa Rica & Central America

03 — Why the Geography Works

Nearshore isn't a discount. It's a design choice.

Companies have moved work to the Americas from as far as South Asia for one reason above all: the workday itself.

Same workday, zero handoffs

Our Mexican hubs run on US Central time — full overlap with every US timezone. Standups, pairing, and same-day answers, not overnight ticket relays.

Proximity you can board

Austin to Monterrey is a two-hour flight, with USMCA-friendly travel. Clients visit their teams for kickoff weeks — our strongest partnerships started face to face.

Cultural fluency

Northern Mexico's business scene is wired to the US market. Engineers join your standups, your Slack, your cadence — without translation layers.

The economics, without the compromise

Regional compensation sits meaningfully below US-market levels — published client work shows around 60% savings versus comparable US hires¹ — while the timezone, and the bar, stay the same. Mexico is the region's premium market; Colombia, Argentina, and the extended network add further headroom.

An employer-side market

Post-pandemic salary spikes have corrected across the region. Qualified candidates are abundant for well-defined roles — which is why clarity on your requirements, not budget alone, drives how fast we staff.
